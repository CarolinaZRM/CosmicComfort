Cosmic Comfort

PURPOSE

CosmicComfort aims to provide users with effective coping mechanisms and personalized aid by offering a range of features. The app provides a comprehensive suite of tools, including calming exercises, self-care reminders, and distraction techniques. By leveraging the accessibility and convenience of mobile platforms, our application is able to provide on-demand, customizable, and user-friendly mental health support, from the comfort and familiarity of the user’s personal mobile device. CosmicComfort aspires to destigmatize mental health, make resources accessible to a wider audience and improve the user’s quality of life.

OBJECTIVES

  1. Develop and integrate at least 5 relaxation techniques, including breathing exercises and calming sounds, by week 15. Ensure each feature is functional, tested, and ready for user interaction, with detailed documentation on implementation.
  2. Set up a system to send self-care reminders using Firebase push notifications by week 15. Ensure the feature is fully operational and includes functionality for users to manage reminder preferences. Document the implementation process and test for reliability and responsiveness.
  3. Ensure the app can handle 500 concurrent users, as measured by stress and load tests. System performance must maintain an average response time of less than 2 seconds per request under full load, with less than a 5% failure rate. These results should be documented and available by week 16 of the project.
  4. Ensure all app pages load within 2 seconds under a minimum load of 300 concurrent users by week 16. Conduct and document load tests, aiming for less than 5% error rate and 90% positive feedback on navigation flow.

PROJECT TIMELINE

  • Research, Planning, and UI/UX Design (Week 5, Sprint 1). Deliverables: Project proposal document.
  • Initial Feature Development(Week 11, Sprint 4). Deliverables: Rudimentary versions of the calming exercises and self-care reminders features, progress report due on October 30, 2024.
  • Final Feature Development(Week 15, Sprint 6). Deliverables: Functional versions of the calming exercises and self-care reminders features.
  • Final Documentation(Week 16, Sprint 7). Deliverables: Finalized version of the codebase documentation, and a finalized, revised version of the final report.
  • Capstone Presentation(Week 17, Sprint 7). Deliverables: Project presentation and demo
